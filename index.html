About IYTO

Empowering the Next Generation of Tech Leaders

The International Youth Tech Olympiad (IYTO) was founded with a singular mission: to discover, nurture, and celebrate the most talented young technologists from every corner of the world.

🌍

Our Mission

To create an inclusive global platform where young innovators can showcase their skills, learn from peers, and build a community that drives positive change through technology.

🎯

Our Vision

A world where every young person, regardless of background, has the opportunity to participate in world-class technology competitions and fulfill their potential.

💡

Our Values

Excellence, innovation, inclusivity, integrity, and collaboration. We believe in fair competition and in lifting each other up as a global community.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · International Youth Tech Olympiad

1. Introduction

The International Youth Tech Olympiad ("IYTO", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website at iyto.online or participate in our competitions.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification: name, date of birth, email address, country of residence
  • Educational information: school or university name, grade/year of study
  • Competition data: category choices, submission files, scores
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and manage your competition registration
  • Communicate important updates, results, and announcements
  • Verify eligibility and prevent fraud
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Issue certificates, medals, and prizes to winners

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers (such as email services and cloud hosting) solely to operate our platform. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

IYTO welcomes participants as young as 12 years old. For participants under 18, we require parental/guardian consent. We take extra care to protect the data of minors and do not use their information for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at iyto.official@hotmail.com.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

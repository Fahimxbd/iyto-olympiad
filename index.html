About IYTO

Empowering
Young Tech Minds

The International Youth Tech Olympiad was founded with one mission: give every young person on earth a fair, free, and world-class platform to discover their potential in technology.

🎯

Our Mission

Create an inclusive global arena where young innovators, regardless of background, can showcase their technical talent.

🌍

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to world-class technology competitions — free of cost, free of barriers.

💡

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, inclusivity, innovation, and youth empowerment through technology.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026 · iyto.online

1. Introduction

IYTO is committed to protecting your personal information when you use iyto.online.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full name, date of birth, email, country
  • Profile picture and bio (optional)
  • School or university name
  • Competition data: answers, scores, progress

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process competition registration and track progress
  • Generate and issue official certificates
  • Send important competition updates via email

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Data is securely stored via Google Firebase.

5. Children's Privacy

IYTO serves participants as young as 10. We take extra care with minors' data and do not use it for marketing.

6. Data Security

All data is encrypted. Firebase industry-standard security. HTTPS enforced across the site.

7. Your Rights

Request data access, correction, or deletion: iyto.official@hotmail.com
